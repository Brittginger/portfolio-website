Healthful

The Project

I wanted to design a responsive application that allows users an easy and effective method to access their coverages and specialists and use them to encourage life habits such a physical training and dietary changes to lead a happier and healthier lifestyle. At the same time, I did not want to create an app that had so much complexity that only a few could understand the app without getting lost

The problem

People need an umbrella app that allows them to choose and stay connected with their in-network health care professionals while being able to create, store, and view the progress of their physical and mental goals because they want to start or continue living a healthier lifestyle easier.

Competitive Analysis

My first priority was to review what was currently on the market and research competitive apps and locate their strengths and weaknesses. I reviewed customer reviews and complaints and spent time using their app myself and where my app could dominate.

User Stories

User Persona One:

As a customer, I want to be able to access my health insurance when I need it, regardless where I am.

User Persona Two:

As a customer, I want to be able to choose or change my specialists and know immediately if they are in network and the cost.

User Persona Three:

As a customer, I want to be able to schedule an appointment with my specialist for any one of my beneficiaries on my phone or computer.

User Flows: Persona Three

Goal: Schedule an appointment

Start: Login

Task List:

  1. Login to Main Page
  2. Choose Specialists from Menu Page
  3. Scroll to desired saved specialist
  4. Click Schedule
  5. Choose type, date, time, and covered individual
  6. Add additional comments as necessary
  7. Click "Schedule"
  8. Click "confirm"
  9. Success!

User Testing

I interviewed 6 volunteers (via Zoom) who answered basic demographic questions. (with informed consent)They were then asked to interact with the prototypes by completing certain tasks and providing feedback and frustrations as they went. The following is are the questions asked and the result.

Resulting Feedback

  • 100% Stated they want an app like Healthful
  • 75% Liked that they could schedule appointments for covered family members as well.
  • 50% Wanted a more social feel to the app
  • 100% Could not immediately locate the insurance information tab.
  • 75% Noticed there was no place to cancel or reschedule appointments

The Resulting Alterations

  1. I relocated the insurance info and benefits to its own location into the profile page and gave it its own space on the main page for easier access.
  2. I added an additional section to scheduled appoinments to allow for rescheduling and canceling.
    * A note will appear during cancellation warning of any possible cancellation fees.
  3. The main page was reorganized to feel less bare and also give it a social feel by adding in local challenges that anyone in the area can participate in.
